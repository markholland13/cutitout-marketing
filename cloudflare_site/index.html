Frequently Asked Questions

answers to the questions customers usually ask before ordering

These cover the common points that slow down first-time orders and help customers decide whether they are ready to upload a file.

What file types do you accept?

The quoting flow is built around DXF files. If you are preparing a job for pricing, start with a clean DXF and check the file guidance before upload.

What materials can I quote online?

Common online options include mild steel, stainless steel, aluminium, and acrylic, with material and thickness selected during quoting.

Can I order one-offs as well as batches?

Yes. The site is set up for one-off parts, repeat jobs, and small production runs, with quantity affecting the price per part.

Can I add powder coating?

Yes, where supported. Finishing can be added during quoting so the total price reflects both cutting and coating requirements.

Do you deliver across the UK?

Yes, the service is aimed at UK mainland delivery, and shipping is included as part of the online order flow.

Can I reorder a part later?

Yes. Repeat ordering is one of the core strengths of the site, so previous jobs can be found again through the account area.
